About Us

Welcome to The decor Shop

SUREKH DECOR Is An Online Retailer Of Luxury, Modern And Elegant Home Décor And Lifestyle Products. Operating From Thane, Mumbai, Maharashtra.
Surekh Décor Philosophy Is That It Is Possible To Feature Well-Designed, Fashion-Forward, High-Quality Products At Competitive Prices. The Four Cornerstones Are :

  • Design: Well-Designed, Fashion-Forward Products For The Mass Market
  • Quality: High-Quality Products Produced In A Quality-Controlled Environment
  • Value: Offering Well-Designed, Quality Products At Affordable Prices
  • Relationships: Listening To Our Buyers And Building Trusting Relationships With Them

Surekh Decor Factory Located In Bhiwandi-Mumbai. The Owners Of Company Are Shubham Patil (Co-Founder) And Umesh Shinde (Co-Founder). We Have Fully Dedicated Team Of Designers, Manufacturing Workers And Marketing. Who Are Working With Utmost Inspiration To Bring You Best Quality And Most Innovative Home Decor Products.

Our Categories Include Designer Lights, Designer Paintings, Tables, Wall Clocks, Planters, Candles, Small Décor Products.
